Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to SocialManager ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Social media account credentials (Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter)
  • Images and captions you create or upload
  • Posting preferences and scheduling information

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage data and analytics
  • Crash reports and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our social media management services
  • Generate captions for your images using AI
  • Schedule and post content to your social media accounts
  • Improve our app functionality and user experience
  • Send you important updates and notifications

4. Social Media Integration

Our app integrates with social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter) to:

  • Post content on your behalf
  • Access your profile information for authentication
  • Schedule posts according to your preferences

We only access the permissions you explicitly grant us.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored locally on your device
  • Social media credentials are encrypted and stored securely
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Facebook SDK: For Facebook and Instagram integration
  • X API: For Twitter/X posting functionality
  • AI Services: For caption generation

These services have their own privacy policies that you should review.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Revoke social media permissions
  • Opt-out of certain data collection

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as you use our app. You can delete your account and data at any time through the app settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
